ABOUT US

Down The Aisle is a full-service design and wedding planning service based in beautiful Nelson, New Zealand. We specialize in unique weddings and exquisite events throughout New Zealand or in any dream destination. Inspired by your personal style and goals, every wedding is executed with efficient planning and the greatest attention to detail. We work tirelessly to design and execute the most unforgettable day of your life. We begin our personalized process by pulling inspiration from you, the couple! We want to know what you like, love and the characteristics of your relationship that make you exceptionally unique and therefore, in turn, we will be producing the amazing framework for the most important day of your lives. Our emphasis is the complete event experience for you and your guests, with every detail exactly planned, tailored and perfect. We want to help make your dreams a reality. This involves a lot of organization, attention to detail and creativity. We would be so honoured to help you plan and begin the journey we call marriage, and make your engagement season as enjoyable as possible.
